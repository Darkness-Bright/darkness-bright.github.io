The End poem: part 4

Conclusion and overall analysis

The poem had no name, but it is always referred to as The End poem. After that the credits would roll. Finally, the player would be spawned back in the game.

And yet, as the players beat the game, they expected just a credits roll, but they got a poem instead. Not just a regular one, but a poem that has the potential to unlock a higher spirituality (I had no better phrase to describe this). The poem was too enigmatic for almost any person, so... they either forgot about the poem literally one minute later or they thought the game was trying to convince them to "get a life".

Some attempts at decoding this poem have been made, and some of them got as deep as deciphering that the poem tells people that life is actually a long dream that you could wake up from...?


The poem is long, but is rather short for something that would change lives forever.

Now to summarize the main points of my analysis:

1. Dreams are everywhere, and could be anything. There are happy dreams and sad dreams, and the only way to not dream is to be fully aware of everything.

2. You are everything. You are the darkness you fight. You are the light you seek...

3. You are love, and your self is all you ever need...

Well, these are the three main points that made the poem very enigmatic! :)
The second of them is the 'ultimate truth'.

Never overlook the magic of these concepts here... You would be surprised what these could do to a person's perceptions and 'spirituality'...
