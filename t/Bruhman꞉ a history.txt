Bruhman was founded in 2772 by King Aeon Eternalis.

The reason why he founded the kingdom at such a peculiar date remains unclear. Many think that it was just a coincidence.

Aeon Eternalis managed to ensure world peace, and subsequently he was able to establish a kingdom so vast that everyone was included in it, and policies so fair-tempered everyone was proud to follow. When the kingdom was established, there was the greatese celebration is history.

The Bruhman council decided that from that point on, a new era should start. The Bruhman era had officially begun.


It was a thousand years After Eternalis. (1000 A.E.) The king was well and the kingdom was well. The people of Bruhman were living peacefully. People have forgotten about war. Those who seek power had never done so unfairly ever again. The stringent yet forgiving policies of Bruhman were taken for granted, and no one has ever gotten unfair treatment ever again... Development of technology and of spiritual practices truly soared.

Exploration and the search for knowledge never ends... right? More and more subjects have been completely mastered... Then, when it was almost 1999 A.E., all development stopped.

All chemical formulae have been discovered; all digits of pi had been found; all limits have been reached. There is no way forward... No.. Way..

It didn't take much time for the people of Bruhman to figure out what this meant. What should people do now?
