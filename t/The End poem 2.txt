1. dream

"dream" has appeared a stunning amount of times, and all its references are recorded below:

- It is reading our thoughts as though they were words on a screen. That is how it chooses to imagine many things, when it is deep in the dream of a game.

What "dream" could mean:
- Real life
- A dream in your sleep
- A hallucination
- A video game or simulation

Notice that these meanings could be stacked recursively.
A hallucination that you are playing a game in your dream would be:

Real life -> Sleeping dream -> Hallucination -> Playing a game
Dream 1   -> Dream 2        -> Dream 3       -> Dream 4

In the situation described, you would be having four dreams at the same time, but only aware of only one of them.
