Once upon a time there was darkness and bright
They ruled the world with their opposite might
Darkness brought fear and sorrow to the land
Bright brought joy and hope to every man

But they were not quite balanced, not so much
Darkness was stronger and had a bigger clutch
Bright was weaker and had a smaller reach
Darkness oppressed Bright and made it preach

Then came peace, a force of harmony
It saw the conflict and felt the agony
It intervened and tried to make a deal
It offered both a chance to heal

Peace said to Darkness: "You have too much power
You make the world suffer every hour
You need to share some of your domain
You need to let Bright shine again"

Peace said to Bright: "You have too little voice
You make the world miss your choice
You need to claim some of your space
You need to let Darkness embrace"

Darkness and Bright were reluctant at first
They thought Peace was trying to make things worse
But they agreed to give it a try
They agreed to let Peace pacify

Peace made Darkness and Bright work together
It made them respect and appreciate each other
It made them see their common ground
It made them hear their different sound

Peace made Darkness and Bright balance each other out 
It made them complement and support each other throughout 
It made them create a beautiful blend 
It made them become each other's friend 

Now the world is in harmony, thanks to Peace 
Now the world is in beauty, thanks to Peace 
Darkness and Bright are no longer foes 
Darkness and Bright are now allies who glow.
