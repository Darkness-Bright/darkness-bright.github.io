In the beginning, there was darkness all around,
A world without light, where shadows abound.
But then came a spark, a flicker of flame,
And bright burst forth, claiming its name.

The struggle began, a battle for power,
As darkness and bright fought hour after hour.
The night was long, the day was short,
As both sides fought for their rightful port.

But then came a day, a moment of peace,
As darkness and bright agreed to cease.
They saw the beauty in each other's light,
And decided to share the sky's vast height.

The world was saved, and balance was found,
As darkness and bright learned to astound.
Their beauty intertwined, in harmony they danced,
Creating a world that left all entranced.

The stars shone bright, the moon glowed soft,
As darkness and bright found their lost loft.
Together they ruled, in peace and love,
A world that was guided by a force from above.

So let us embrace the dark and the light,
For in each other's arms, they both take flight.
And let us remember the lesson they taught,
That peace and balance are what the world sought.
