In the depths of night, darkness reigns supreme,
A shroud of black, devoid of any beam.
But in the distance, a beacon of light,
A glimmer of hope, a fleeting sight.

The two forces clash, in endless strife,
One seeking to end, the other bring life.
The darkness swells, consuming all,
As the light flickers, ready to fall.

But then a change, a subtle shift,
As both sides realize, their endless rift.
Peace descends, upon the land,
Balancing darkness, with a gentle hand.

The light shines bright, but not too much,
The darkness recedes, but not as such.
A harmony found, between the two,
A perfect balance, forever true.

Now the world is saved, from eternal night,
And the light and darkness, dance in sight.
Together they flourish, in perfect blend,
A beautiful harmony, that will never end.
